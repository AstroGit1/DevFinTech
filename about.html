About Us

Welcome to DevFintech, the exciting new division of Youth Coding Workshops! Our mission is to spread financial literacy and fintech knowledge to young minds across the globe and to enclose the digital divide. Our non-profit has already impacted over 2,500 students and we are eager to expand further with this branch.

Students will join weekly meetings to learn more about topics in Financial Literacy/Fintech. A class will normally consist of a broad overview of the topics and then a programming assignment related to the subject.

Our funding is dedicated to purchasing electronics and accessories for underprivileged students, ensuring they have the tools to participate in our workshops and gain vital skills.
